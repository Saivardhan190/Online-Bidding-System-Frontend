Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a bidder?

After creating an account, go to "Become a Bidder" section and submit your application. Admin will review and approve your request within 24-48 hours.

Is there a registration fee?

No, creating an account and applying as a bidder is completely free. You only pay when you win an auction.

How does live bidding work?

Once an auction starts, you can place bids in real-time. The highest bidder when the timer ends wins the stall. You'll receive instant notifications if someone outbids you.

What happens after I win?

You'll receive a confirmation email with payment instructions and stall allocation details. Complete the payment within the specified time to confirm your booking.
